# R100-Privacy-Policy
Last updated: 15/11/2025

R100 – Rule of 100 Tracker (“the App”) is a simple productivity tool designed to help you track your daily actions. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

1. Data we collect
The App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable data.

2. Data stored on your device
All targets, progress, and usage data are stored locally on your device. This information is used only to show your progress inside the App. We do not have access to this data.

3. Third-party services
R100 does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or any third-party services that collect personal data inside the App.

4. Children’s privacy
The App is not designed for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

5. Changes to this policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.

6. Contact
If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how the App handles data, you can contact us at:
support@amrania.com
